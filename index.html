About us

Crescendo is the most prestigious society of Amity Mayur Vihar, it is also one of the biggest Western Music societies in all of Delhi. It is a place where every musician belongs and is one of the biggest platforms to display one's talents. Everybody here gets an equal opportunity to showcase their talents and compete to prove themselves.
