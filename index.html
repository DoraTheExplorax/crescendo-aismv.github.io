About us

Crescendo is the Western Music Society of Amity International School Mayur Vihar. One of the most prestigious societies of our school, Crescendo has been organising wonderful events for 4 years now. We are a diverse team of 20-25 students, each one unique in themselves. From talented instrumentalists to budding vocalists, orators and artists, we have got it all. Each giving their best, our cohesive effort has resulted in many successful events and shows.
