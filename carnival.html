Winter Carnival

DJ stall, adding a touch of music to the carnival each year

Festive Treat

Winter Carnival is a great way to kick start the winter festivities and, what other way to make it even more special than an energetic DJ session. The barnstorming lineup of songs will get you on the dance floor for sure.

The Stall

One of the most visited stall at the Carnival, the DJ stall is a popular demand. With tunes you can't get enough of, the dance session will you leave full of vim and vigor.

The DJ

The show stopper of the event would definitely be the DJ session where the professional DJ plays grooving and effervescent tunes, giving everyone an opportunity to show off their infamous dance moves.
