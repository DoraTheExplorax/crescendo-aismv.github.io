Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2019-2020

Ayan Sharma - President

7 times best drummer, Ayan has been an integral part of the society for the past 4 years. Apart from being an impeccable percussionist, his amicable personality has always made him very approachable. Perfect fit for the this post, Ayan is very dedicated to music.

Karthik Suresh - Vice President

He is an exceptional bassist. He has contributed to the band for the past 3 years and has won 3 individual prizes for the best bassist. One of the best bassists in the Delhi Circuit, Karthik's quality of perseverance has always aided the band as well as the society in numerous ways.

Jasleen Kaur - Secretary

Contributing at each step of the way, Jasleen has been a part of the society since it's very beginning. Gifted with a creative mind, her artistic strokes leave one and all spellbound. Contributing not just her voice in the choir, but also her management skills to the society, Jasleen's dedication is unparalleled.
