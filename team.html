Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2019-2020

Armaan Afzal - President

President for two consecutive years, Armaan Afzal is an exceptional vocalist and an articulate orator. Blessed with a charismatic personality, Armaan has won numerous laurels for our school. His immaculate organisational skills are our society's pillar of strength.

Shagun Malhotra - Vice President

Shagun Malhotra is blessed with a mellifluous voice. With her spot-on harmonies, Shagun has represented our school and won us numerous award in various competitions. Along with this, her superb management skills have many-a-times played a major role in successfully carrying out many events.

Devashish Gupta - Secretary

Devashish has been a valuable part of our society. Gifted with amazing guitar skills, he is an integral part of our school band. His quality of staying clear headed under pressure has aided the society in conducting many uninterrupted and successful shows.
